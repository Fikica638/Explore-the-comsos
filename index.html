Planet of our solar system



  1. Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and it is also the smallest in our solar system
  2. Venus is the second planet in our solar system, it's atmosphere is made from toxic gases so it is not habitable.
  3. Earth is the most habitable planet ever, it is made out of water and land and it has the moon orbiting it.
  4. Mars is hope to the largest mountain ever seen/recorded. Olympus Mons in 4x bigger than Mount Everest.

  6. Saturn is the second biggest planet in our solar system and it is known for it's rings.
  7. Uranus is the only planet in our solar system made out of ice, that is why it's got it's nickname: The ice giant.
  8. Neptune is the lonliest planet in our solar system, it is known for it's supersonic deadly winds and icy climate.
